Meet the Organisers

PakSolve is run by Pakistani undergrads at MIT who have represented Pakistan at International Math and Physics Olympiads. Here's who's behind the program.

Talha Ashraf

Talha is a first year Physics major at MIT and a Bronze medalist in the 2024 International Physics Olympiad. He will be the head student mentor at PakSolve

Osman Siddique

Osman is a third year CS student at MIT. He won an honorable mention in 2021 and a Bronze medal in 2022 at the International Physics Olympiad.

Bilal Asmatullah

Bilal is a third year CS student at MIT. He won a Bronze medal in 2022 and an honorable mention in 2023 at the International Physics Olympiad.

Ahmed Raza

Ahmed is a second year Math student at NTU Singapore. He won a Bronze medal at the 2023 International Mathematics Olympiad.

Daniyal Shahzad

Daniyal won a Bronze medal at the 2024 International Physics Olympiad in France, and is on the current Pakistan IPhO team.

Ehban Awan

Ehban is a second year Physics and CS major at UCLA. He won a Bronze medal in the 2024 International Nuclear Science Olympiad.
