What is PakSolve?

The Pakistan STEM Problem Solving Talent Hunt (PakSolve for short) is a free virtual program for curious, ambitious Pakistani students who are interested in gaining a high-quality education in Math and Science. At PakSolve, you will be instructed by MIT students and Pakistanis who have won medals at the International Physics and Math Olympiads. By signing up, you will become part of a large community of similarly ambitious Pakistani students so that you can all learn together and collaborate to solve fun, challenging problems.
